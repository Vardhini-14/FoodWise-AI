General Nutritional Tips

  • Focus on whole, unprocessed foods.
  • Stay well-hydrated throughout the day.
  • Include a variety of fruits and vegetables.
  • Incorporate lean proteins and healthy fats.
  • Practice mindful eating and portion control.
  • Combine healthy eating with regular physical activity.
  • Ensure adequate sleep for overall well-being.
  • Cook more at home to control ingredients.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Limit added sugars and unhealthy fats.

Healthy Food Swaps

Discover smarter choices for common ingredients and dishes.

White Rice Quinoa/Brown Rice

Higher fiber, more protein, and complex carbohydrates for sustained energy.

Sour Cream Greek Yogurt

Lower fat, higher protein, and beneficial probiotics.

Mayonnaise Avocado Mash/Hummus

Healthier fats, fiber, and fewer processed ingredients.

Sugary Sodas Infused Water/Sparkling Water

Eliminate added sugars and artificial sweeteners, stay hydrated naturally.

White Bread Whole Wheat Bread/Lettuce Wraps

More fiber and nutrients for better digestion and satiety.

Potato Chips Air-popped Popcorn/Baked Veggie Chips

Less unhealthy fats and sodium, more fiber.

Processed Snacks Fruits/Nuts

Natural vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats instead of artificial additives.

Fried Chicken Grilled Chicken

Significantly less fat and calories, while maintaining protein content.
